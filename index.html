Services

Types Of Services We Offer

Repair Services

We Offer rewinding and repairing of AC / DC / LT Motors / Alternators / Turbo Alternator / Servo Motors / AC Slipring Motors / Transformers etc. We also offer repairing of fabricated control panel, Automatic Voltage Regulator, Battery Charging Device, Generator Control Panel, Voltage Monitoring Relay, Vingtor Marine Telephone Unit Board and many more. Click on button below to learn more about it.

Manufacturing

We Do manufacturing of fabricated control panel as per application with gaurantee of performance. We also manufacture many other types of minute electrical and electronics component used in various parts of vessels for various functions.  All are products are manufactured with International Quality Standards and checks. Click on button below to learn more about it.

Our Products

We are retailer of Automatic Voltage Regulator, Battery Charging Device, Generator Control Panel, Voltage Monitoring Relay, Vingtor Marine Telephone Unit Board and many more. All our offered products are thoroughly manufactured at our vendors end under the guidance of quality controllers using best raw material and sound technology in adherence to quality norms.
