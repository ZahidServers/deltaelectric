About

We are here to provide services which focus on Customer Centricity rather than Profit making motive. If You join us then You get the transparency and customer satisfaction with right guidance of our services along with the trust seal of Delta Electric Services.

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    After the dissolution of Favourite Offshore due we restarted our company again in 2010 as Delta Electric recapturing our market stakes with main focus on AVR manifacturing, Generator speed controller, application enineering, retro fitting, control card repairing, control card manifacturing, Motor Rewinding etc.

  • March 2011

    Expansion into Retail Segment

    We expanded into retail market of manifactured products by other companies as well such as AVR, Circuit Breakers, Generator Conrol Panels, Shaft Generator Converter Panels, Battery Charging Devices,DC to Dc Converter, I to P Covertors, ABB AC Drives,ABB DC Drives,Bilege ALarms, ACBs,AC/DC Motor Panels, ACBs, Step Up/Down Transformers, Water Level Sensors, AC/DC Voltage Meters.

  • December 2015

    Online Presence

    From here on we started listing our retail products on indiamart and other sites. It was our first time experience with any B2B online platform but we decided to go for it. Basically it was our first step towards our online presence which we wanted to take futher.

  • June 2021

    Phase Two Expansion

    From here on we decided to launch our own website and sell our products online platforms like amazon for the ease of our customers. We hired Zahid Servers on of the best IT field for this job. Soon we will launching our app also.
